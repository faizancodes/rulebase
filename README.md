## Overview

Rulebase is a federal regulatory intelligence platform for compliance teams, policy analysts, and legal researchers. It brings together Federal Register notices, Congress.gov bill activity, and SEC EDGAR filings to help users understand what government actions are happening, how they connect across sources, and which developments are most likely to matter to public companies.

The platform is designed to turn fragmented federal signals into an operational monitoring system. Users can search, compare, and track regulatory activity across agencies, committees, issuers, industries, and keywords while surfacing timelines, cross-references, and compliance impact signals in one workspace.

## Features

- Federal Register, Congress.gov, and SEC filing monitoring
- Cross-source search and comparison
- Regulatory timeline and velocity views
- Compliance impact scoring and dependency mapping
- Alerts and saved searches
- Entity-focused monitoring for agencies, issuers, and committees
